Leg 1

Java Adventure — Apr 9-16

The Plan

Arrive Bali → 1 night Canggu (drop wedding gear at storage) → Ferry to Java → Kawah Ijen blue flame trek → Optional Mt Bromo sunrise → Surabaya sightseeing + family archives → Fly back to Bali Apr 16

Day 1: April 9 (Thu) — Arrival

  • Arrive Bali (Ngurah Rai Airport) — eSIM already active
  • Grab to Canggu (~45 min - 1 hour)
  • Drop wedding gear at Bali Safe Storage (Jl. Raya Canggu No.165) — balisafestorage.id — WhatsApp: +62 811-3806-911
  • Check into 2-bedroom villa nearby
  • Dinner in Canggu (Old Man's, The Lawn, Batu Bolong area)

Day 2: April 10 (Fri) — Transit to Java

  • Check out, private driver to Gilimanuk ferry (~3.5-4 hrs, ~$77 AUD total)
  • Ferry Gilimanuk → Ketapang (30-45 min, $0.60/person)
  • Continue to Ijen area (~1.5 hrs)
  • Check into hotel near Ijen

Day 3: April 11 (Sat) — Kawah Ijen Blue Flame Trek

  • Midnight pickup from hotel
  • Drive to Paltuding trailhead (~1 hr)
  • Trek to crater (3km, 1.5-2 hrs up)
  • BLUE FLAMES visible until ~4:30 AM
  • Sunrise over turquoise crater lake
  • Descend, return to hotel, rest
  • Afternoon: explore Banyuwangi or visit Jagir Waterfall
Trek tips: Gas mask required (~$4.50 AUD rental). Bring warm layers (2,386m elevation), sturdy shoes, headlamp. Entrance ~$10-15 AUD.

Day 4: April 12 (Sun) — Bromo or Surabaya

Option A: Direct to Surabaya (train/bus/shuttle)

Option B (recommended): Drive to Mt Bromo area (~5-6 hrs), rest for early morning sunrise jeep tour

Day 5: April 13 (Mon) — Bromo Sunrise or Surabaya

  • If Bromo: 3am jeep to King Kong Hill viewpoint, sunrise over crater, climb 253 steps, then drive to Surabaya (~3-4 hrs)
  • If Surabaya: Explore — House of Sampoerna, Submarine Monument, Arab Quarter

Days 6-7: April 14-15 — Surabaya

  • City exploration: House of Sampoerna, Arab Quarter & Ampel Mosque, local markets
  • Family history archives at Jl. Jagir Wonokromo No. 350 (colonial records research)
  • Shopping, food, culture

Day 8: April 16 (Thu) — Fly Back to Bali

  • Garuda SUB → DPS (9:20am, ~1 hr flight) Booked
  • Collect wedding gear from storage
  • Check into Theanna Kirana Villa
  • Dom's 33rd birthday!

Dom's Solo Flores Heritage Trip — Apr 29 to May 10

This section is Dom-only. Buddha, you're on a plane home by now. But have a read if you're curious about the family history adventure!

The Mission

Tracing the roots of James Peter Subtino Solien ("Jimmy Malay") — Dom's ancestor, a maritime trader who likely originated from eastern Flores (Larantuka/Sikka area). Looking for connections in the old Portuguese-indigenous Catholic communities.

Local Transport in Bali & Java

  • Grab / Gojek — the Uber of SE Asia. Download both apps. Use for all rides.
  • No scooter rentals (no QLD motorcycle licence)
  • Cash for ferries and rural areas. ATMs limited in mountain areas.
